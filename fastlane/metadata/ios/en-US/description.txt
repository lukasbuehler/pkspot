THE SPOT FOR EVERYTHING PARKOUR.

PK Spot helps you find places to train, check in at spots, discover what is happening nearby, and connect with your local parkour community.

FIND SPOTS
Explore parkour spots around you with photos, ratings, tags, and filters for indoor, covered, lit, dry, water, and purpose-built training places.

CHECK IN
Let the community know where training is active and keep a lightweight history of the spots you visit.

DISCOVER WHAT IS HAPPENING
Find events, jams, and community pages as they are added, so it is easier to see where people train and gather.

BUILT WITH THE COMMUNITY
PK Spot is open source and community driven. Spot data contributed by the community is shared under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 so active parkour communities can build on it.

PK Spot started in Zurich and is growing toward one app for parkour spots, training, events, jams, and community.
